Boarding that suits your needs

Our Boarding School community offers an exceptional, well-rounded education. Every student is provided with clear behavioral expectations, a nurturing learning environment, and a variety of engaging weekend activities set within the Karuguru region. Boarding teachers and supervising staff create a safe, secure atmosphere every day, ensuring that students enjoy a top-tier experience both during school hours and in the comfort of the boarding home.

Why choose our boarding school

Boarding students benefit from after-school study support and exclusive out-of-hours access to the School's facilities. Our young residents are welcomed into boarding life and positively contribute to their community while also enjoying additional time with their peers.

At our School, the boarding environment cultivates discipline, independent learning skills, and respect for others residing in student accommodation. After homework is completed, students may enjoy their free time with friends in the comfort of the boarding home and build life-long friendships that last beyond their school years.

Boarders learn under direct supervision of experienced educators and House Supervisors. A committed team of adults attends to students around the clock. They create an extended school family, and foster various values in boarding students, including independence, a sense of responsibility, community spirit, organisation, and resilience, developing children into bright, young individuals.

Boarding Activities

Our school offers a vibrant and dynamic boarding experience where students thrive both academically and socially. During weekends, the boarding side comes alive with exciting activities that cater to diverse interests. Students gather for movie nights, enjoy popular films with friends, and take part in energetic dance sessions that keep everyone active and entertained. On Sundays, the community participates in uplifting church services, fostering a spirit of togetherness and reflection. Sports enthusiasts engage in friendly matches, showcasing their talents and teamwork. These activities, along with many others, ensure that every weekend is filled with fun, relaxation, and personal growth.

Meals and Other services

Daily meals are provided to boarders. They are nutritious, healthy and delicious. Children are regularly consulted to ensure their satisfaction at meal times. Breakfast, lunch and dinner is served in the Boarding House Dining Hall during out-of-school hours. In addition to daily meal provision, the Boarding School also provides special meals at times. Church services are organised on Sundays for those who would like to attend.
