A Premier Choice for Primary Education

At our Primary School, we provide a nurturing and dynamic environment where every child is empowered to flourish. From Play Group to Class 3, students are supported with personalised attention, a broad curriculum, and engaging activities designed to inspire a lifelong love of learning. We celebrate the individuality of each learner while ensuring they build a strong academic and emotional foundation for future success.

Primary School Options

We offer flexible entry points across Play Group, PP1, PP2, Class 1, Class 2, and Class 3. Full-time attendance ensures students benefit from the full range of learning experiences, while optional aftercare services are available for families who require additional support. Our inclusive environment supports smooth transitions between early years and lower primary levels.

Our Approach to Learning

Our Primary School embraces a child-centred, inquiry-based approach to education, encouraging curiosity, independence, and collaboration. We believe children thrive when they are active participants in their learning, guided by teachers who nurture their creativity, critical thinking, and communication skills.

Student Support and Well-being

Our Primary School places strong emphasis on student well-being, ensuring each child feels valued and confident. Teachers provide individualised support, and social-emotional learning is integrated into daily life. We foster a culture of kindness, respect, and resilience to help students navigate their growing world.
