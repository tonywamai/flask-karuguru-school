Building Champions through sports

Our school offers a rich and inclusive sports programme designed to develop physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and a lifelong love for healthy living. Students participate in a variety of sports including football, athletics, swimming, basketball, and more.Whether playing competitively or for recreation, every child is encouraged to discover their strengths and enjoy the thrill of sport. Through inter-house and inter-school competitions, students not only sharpen their athletic skills but also learn resilience, leadership, and the value of good sportsmanship.

Football

Football is one of the most popular and well-supported sports in our school, with both boys' and girls' teams actively participating. Training sessions are held regularly under the guidance of skilled coaches who focus on developing technical skills, tactical awareness, and teamwork.Our teams compete in friendly matches and inter-school tournaments, giving students the opportunity to showcase their talent, build confidence, and learn the importance of discipline and sportsmanship. Basketball

Basketball is a thriving sport in our school, played with great enthusiasm by both boys and girls. Our dedicated teams train regularly to focus on skill development, teamwork, and strategy.Students participate in internal leagues and inter-school competitions, which help boost their confidence, coordination, and leadership qualities. Basketball not only promotes physical fitness but also teaches resilience, quick decision-making, and respect.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a dynamic and engaging sport at our school, embraced by both boys and girls across various grades. The game encourages quick thinking, precision, and strong communication skills, making it a favourite for students who enjoy fast-paced team play.Our volleyball teams benefit from structured training sessions and friendly matches that focus on building technical skills like serving, passing, and spiking, while also fostering teamwork and trust among players. Whether in friendly competitions or spirited inter-house games, volleyball provides a fun and inclusive environment for students to grow both socially and athletically.
