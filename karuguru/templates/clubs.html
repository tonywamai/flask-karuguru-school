Unleashing Potential Beyond the Classroom

We believe that learning goes far beyond textbooks. Every student participates in a wide range of clubs and activities designed to nurture creativity, teamwork, leadership, and individual talents. From music and drama to science, environmental conservation,agriculture, sports, and art, our co-curricular programme offers something for everyone. These clubs not only complement academic learning but also help students discover new passions, build lifelong skills, and develop a strong sense of belonging. Participation is encouraged across all grades to promote a well-rounded, confident learner.

Music and Drama Club

Our Music and Drama Club is a dynamic and creative space where students express themselves through rhythm, melody, and performance. The club plays a central role in school life, offering learners the chance to explore their talents through various musical instruments such as piano, guitar, drums, recorder, and percussion. Guided by skilled instructors, students develop both vocal and instrumental proficiency while building confidence in public performance.Dramatic arts are equally emphasized, with learners participating in role play, storytelling, and stage performances that enhance communication skills and imagination. The club hosts regular showcases and school events, giving students exciting opportunities to shine and share their talents with the wider community.

Agricultural Club

The Agricultural Club provides students with hands-on experience in sustainable farming and environmental care. Through engaging, practical activities such as planting vegetables , maintaining small gardens, and managing compost systems, learners gain a deeper understanding of where food comes from and the importance of responsible land use. Through supervision, students learn key agricultural skills including soil preparation, crop care, irrigation methods, and organic farming practices. The club also promotes environmental stewardship by encouraging recycling, tree planting, and awareness of climate-smart agriculture.

Journalism Club

The Journalism Club empowers students to become confident communicators and critical thinkers. Members explore the art of storytelling, reporting, and creative writing while learning how to gather information, conduct interviews, and produce engaging content. From school newsletters and blogs to event coverage and photography, students gain hands-on experience in various media formats

Home Science Club

This Club equips students with essential life skills through fun in areas such as cooking, sewing, nutrition, and home management. Members learn how to prepare simple, healthy meals, maintain cleanliness and hygiene, and create useful household items through crafts and fabric work.

Dancing

The Dancing Club is a lively and energetic space where students express themselves through movement and rhythm. Learners are introduced to a variety of dance styles, including traditional, contemporary, and cultural dances that celebrate diversity and creativity.Through regular practice, choreography, and performances at school events, members build confidence, coordination, and teamwork. The club promotes physical fitness, self-expression, and a strong sense of community as students dance their way to joy and self-discovery.
